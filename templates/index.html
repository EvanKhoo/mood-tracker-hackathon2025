What You Want - When You Want It

Reflexion believes music isn’t just sound — it’s a force that shapes your mood, focus, and perspective. We’re building a way to make that power effortless. Our mood-sensing bracelet connects directly to your Spotify, adapting your music in real time to match how you feel — or how you want to feel. Music can lift moods, ease stress, and reshape outlooks. We’re making that power yours to control.

Reflexion was born from a simple idea — music should move with you. Your energy, your focus, your mood — they change every moment. So we built a way for your music to change with them. Reflexion is a mood-sensing bracelet that connects with Spotify to create a living soundtrack — one that shifts with how you feel, and guides you toward how you want to feel. Because music shouldn’t just play to you. It should play with you.
